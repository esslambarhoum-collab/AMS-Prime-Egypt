Simple & Transparent

How We Export

A clear four-step process from sourcing to delivery, fully documented at every stage.

1🔍

Source & Verify

We source directly from Egyptian producers and manufacturers, verifying quality standards before any commitment.

2🔬

Certify & Test

Every product is tested by EGAC-accredited laboratories. Full COA, phytosanitary and origin certificates prepared.

3📦

Document & Clear

We handle all export documentation, customs clearance and compliance with Egyptian export regulations.

4🚢

Ship & Deliver

Land freight via Saudi Arabia or sea freight to your nearest GCC port, with full cargo insurance on every shipment.

Why Work With Us
