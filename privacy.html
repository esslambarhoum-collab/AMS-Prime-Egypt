2 What Data We Collect

We only collect data that you voluntarily provide to us through our website enquiry form. We do not collect data automatically through tracking pixels, behavioural analytics, or advertising technologies.

Data you provide when submitting an enquiry:

  • Full name
  • Company name
  • Email address
  • WhatsApp number or telephone number
  • Product(s) of interest
  • Destination country
  • Estimated order quantity
  • Any additional information you choose to include in the notes field

Technical data collected automatically by your browser:

  • Your IP address (collected by our web hosting provider — GitHub Pages — for security purposes)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time of visit

We do not operate advertising campaigns, install tracking cookies of our own, or use your data for any purpose other than responding to your business enquiry.

We Do Not Collect We do not collect payment card details, government identification numbers, health data, or any special category data. Do not submit such information through this website.

3 How We Use Your Data

We use the personal data you provide solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your business enquiry — including providing product specifications, pricing quotations, and relevant export documentation
  • To maintain records of business communications — as required for legitimate commercial operations
  • To contact you regarding your specific enquiry — via email or WhatsApp as indicated in your submission
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations — including Egyptian commercial law and export regulations

We will not use your data for:

  • Unsolicited marketing or promotional communications
  • Selling or renting your data to any third party
  • Profiling, automated decision-making, or behavioural advertising
  • Any purpose unrelated to your original enquiry without your explicit consent

4 Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent — By submitting our enquiry form and ticking the consent checkbox, you expressly consent to us processing your data to respond to your enquiry
  • Legitimate interests — We have a legitimate business interest in maintaining records of commercial enquiries and communications for a reasonable period
  • Legal obligation — We may be required to retain certain business records under applicable Kuwaiti commercial law and Egyptian export regulations

If you are located in a jurisdiction covered by the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or the UK GDPR, you have the right to withdraw consent at any time by contacting us at info@amsprimeegypt.com.

5 Who We Share Your Data With

We treat your personal data as confidential business information. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Internal team members — employees or authorised contractors of AMS Prime Egypt who need access to respond to your enquiry
  • Technology service providers — our website is hosted on GitHub Pages (Microsoft). Our internal CRM may use Supabase (cloud database). Both operate under their own privacy policies and security standards. We do not grant these providers permission to use your data for any other purpose
  • Legal authorities — if required by Egyptian law, court order, or valid legal process, we may disclose data to competent authorities

No other sharing occurs without your prior written consent.

Third-Party Service Providers Used GitHub Pages (website hosting) — github.com/privacy  ·  Supabase (CRM database, if activated) — supabase.com/privacy  ·  Google Fonts (typography, loaded from Google's servers) — policies.google.com/privacy

6 How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, and in accordance with applicable legal requirements:

  • Active enquiries — retained for the duration of the commercial relationship and for 3 years thereafter, consistent with applicable Kuwaiti and Egyptian commercial record-keeping obligations
  • Closed or inactive enquiries — retained for a maximum of 3 years from the date of last contact, after which they are permanently deleted
  • Technical/server logs — retained by our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) in accordance with their own retention policies, typically 90 days

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. See Section 9 for your rights.

AMS Prime Egypt shall not be liable for any unauthorised access resulting from circumstances beyond our reasonable control, including but not limited to cyberattacks, third-party infrastructure failures, or force majeure events.

8 Cookies & Third-Party Services

Our website does not use first-party cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising purposes.

Third-party resources that may set cookies or make network requests:

  • Google Fonts — we load typefaces from Google's servers (fonts.googleapis.com and fonts.gstatic.com). Google may collect your IP address and browser information when loading these fonts. See Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy

  • Unsplash — product images on this website are loaded directly from Unsplash's servers (images.unsplash.com). Unsplash may log your IP address when loading these images

We do not install advertising cookies, social media tracking pixels, or behavioural analytics tools. We do not use Google Analytics or similar services on this website.

By using this website, you acknowledge the above third-party network requests. If you wish to prevent them, you may use a browser-level content blocker.
