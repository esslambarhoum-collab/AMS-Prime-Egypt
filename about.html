Egypt's Premier
Export Company

AMS Prime Egypt For Trading and Export is a registered Egyptian company built on a simple mission: connecting the Gulf and global markets with Egypt's finest products. From premium agricultural produce to confectionery, beverages and consumer goods, we are Egypt's multi-sector export partner.

We operate on the ground in Egypt, sourcing directly from manufacturers and producers, handling laboratory certification, export documentation, logistics coordination, and full compliance with Egyptian and international trade regulations. Our buyers receive a single point of contact for everything.

Quality is non-negotiable. Every agricultural product we export is tested by EGAC-accredited laboratories with full COA provided per shipment. For all other products, we work only with certified manufacturers whose quality standards meet GCC market requirements.
